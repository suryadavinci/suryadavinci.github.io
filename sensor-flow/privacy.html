Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

Quick Summary

Sensor Flow displays real-time data from your device sensors. All sensor data stays on your device ‚Äî we never collect, store, or transmit it. We use Google AdMob for ads in the free version.

Data We Access

Sensor Data (Local Only)

The app accesses the following sensors to display real-time information:

  • Motion Sensors: Accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, barometer
  • Camera/LiDAR: Depth information from device sensors
  • Location: GPS coordinates, speed, altitude, heading
  • Microphone: Audio level metering (decibel values only)
  • Device Sensors: Ambient light, proximity, thermal state

Important: All sensor data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any sensor data.

Third-Party Services

The free version uses Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect device identifiers and usage data. You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings or upgrade to Pro to remove all ads.

Permissions

  • Camera: LiDAR and depth sensing
  • Location: GPS coordinates and speed
  • Microphone: Audio level monitoring
  • Motion: Accelerometer and gyroscope data
  • Tracking: (Optional) Personalized advertisements

You can revoke any permission in iOS Settings at any time.

Your Rights

  • Opt-Out: Decline tracking or disable in Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking
  • Remove Ads: Upgrade to Pro ($9.99)
  • Delete Data: Uninstalling removes all local data

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We never see or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

Sensor Flow is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from children.
