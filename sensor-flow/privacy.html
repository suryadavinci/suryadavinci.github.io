Privacy Policy

Sensor Flow
Last Updated: January 6, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Sensor Flow ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Quick Summary: Sensor Flow accesses device sensors to display real-time data on your device. We do not collect or store personal information on our servers. Device sensor data stays on your device. We use third-party services (Google AdMob) for advertising, which may collect device identifiers.

Information We Collect

1. Sensor Data (Not Collected)

The App accesses the following device sensors to display real-time information:

  • Motion Sensors: Accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, and barometer data
  • Camera/LiDAR: Depth information from the device camera and LiDAR sensor (on supported devices)
  • Location: GPS coordinates, speed, altitude, and heading
  • Microphone: Audio level metering (decibel measurements only)
  • Device Sensors: Ambient light, proximity, and thermal state

Important: All sensor data is processed and displayed locally on your device in real-time. We do not transmit, store, or collect any sensor data on our servers.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

The free version of Sensor Flow displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA - Identifier for Advertisers)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data for ad targeting

You can control ad tracking through your device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking). Our App complies with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and will request your permission before tracking.

Firebase Analytics (Optional)

We may use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This service may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash reports for debugging
  • Device model and OS version

No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.

How We Use Your Information

The data accessed by Sensor Flow is used solely for the following purposes:

  1. App Functionality: To display real-time sensor data on your device screen
  2. Advertising: To show relevant advertisements in the free version (via Google AdMob)
  3. App Improvement: To analyze crashes and improve app performance (via Firebase Analytics)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.

Data Storage and Security

Local Processing: All sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

No Account Required: The App does not require user registration or account creation. We do not maintain user databases.

Third-Party Security: Data collected by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase) is governed by their respective privacy policies and security measures.

Permissions Requested

Sensor Flow requests the following iOS permissions:

  • Camera: To access LiDAR sensor and display depth information
  • Location When In Use: To display GPS coordinates, speed, and altitude
  • Microphone: To monitor and display ambient sound levels
  • Motion & Fitness: To access accelerometer, gyroscope, and barometer data
  • Tracking: To show personalized advertisements (you can decline)

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device Settings. The App will gracefully handle denied permissions and disable related features.

In-App Purchases

Sensor Flow offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Pro Version") that removes advertisements and unlocks premium features. Purchase information is processed through Apple's App Store and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

Sensor Flow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of inappropriate data collection from minors.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our App may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy (AdMob)
  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to access
  • Opt-Out of Tracking: Decline the tracking permission prompt or disable in iOS Settings
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all local data
  • Ad Personalization: Control through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. Since we do not collect personal information, these rights are automatically satisfied.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR. Our legal basis for processing is "legitimate interest" for advertising and "consent" for tracking. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying an in-app notice (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
