Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why do I need to grant permissions?

A: Sensor Flow requires permissions to access device sensors. The app requests only the permissions necessary for displaying sensor data:

  • Camera: For LiDAR and depth sensing features
  • Location: For GPS coordinates, speed, and altitude
  • Microphone: For audio level monitoring
  • Motion: For accelerometer and gyroscope data

You can manage permissions in iOS Settings ‚Üí Sensor Flow.

Q: Is my data being collected or shared?

A: No. All sensor data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not collect, store, or share your sensor data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Why doesn't LiDAR work on my device?

A: LiDAR scanning requires a device with a LiDAR sensor. Compatible devices include iPhone 12 Pro and later Pro models, and iPad Pro (2020 and later). Standard iPhone models do not have LiDAR hardware.

Q: Why is the app draining my battery?

A: Sensor Flow continuously reads and displays real-time sensor data, which requires active processing. To conserve battery:

  • Close the app when not in use (sensors automatically stop)
  • Reduce screen brightness
  • Disable sensors you're not actively monitoring
  • Use Low Power Mode in iOS Settings

Q: How accurate are the sensor readings?

A: Sensor Flow displays raw data directly from Apple's sensor APIs. Accuracy depends on your device's hardware calibration and environmental conditions. For precise scientific measurements, consider professional-grade equipment.

Q: Can I export or record sensor data?

A: The current version displays real-time data only. Data export and recording features may be added in future updates based on user feedback.

Q: How does the Metal Detector feature work?

A: The Metal Detector mode uses your device's magnetometer to detect changes in magnetic field strength. When metal objects are nearby, the magnetic field increases. This feature works best with ferromagnetic metals (iron, steel, nickel).

Q: What does the Bubble Level show?

A: The Bubble Level uses accelerometer data to show your device's tilt in degrees. It helps you align surfaces horizontally or vertically, similar to a traditional carpenter's level.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force quit the app and restart it
  • Restart your iPhone/iPad
  • Ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  • Check that you have sufficient storage space

Sensor Not Displaying Data

  • Check that permissions are granted in iOS Settings ‚Üí Sensor Flow
  • Verify your device supports the sensor (e.g., LiDAR requires Pro models)
  • Try toggling the sensor permission off and back on
  • Restart the app

Location Not Updating

  • Ensure Location Services are enabled: Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Location Services
  • Grant "While Using the App" permission to Sensor Flow
  • Check that you have a clear view of the sky (GPS requires satellite visibility)
  • Wait 30-60 seconds for initial GPS lock

In-App Purchase Issues

  • Verify you're signed into your Apple ID
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app to restore purchases
  • If purchase didn't unlock Pro, tap "Restore Purchase" in Settings
  • Contact Apple Support for App Store billing issues
