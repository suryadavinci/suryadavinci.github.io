Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to LED Neon Banner ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

LED Neon Banner is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app to improve performance and user experience.
  • Ad Tracking (Optional): With your explicit permission, we collect advertising identifiers for personalized ads. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase information is processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details.

Data Storage

All your banner designs, settings, and customizations are stored locally on your device. We do not store this information on our servers or share it with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device and usage information. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: For app analytics and performance monitoring. Firebase data is used to improve app stability and user experience.
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases securely.

App Tracking Transparency

We use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) to request your permission before tracking your data across apps and websites. This allows us to:

  • Show you personalized advertisements
  • Avoid showing you the same ads repeatedly

You can change your tracking preferences at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it for support
  • Location data
  • Photos or other media from your device
  • Contacts or other sensitive personal information

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads through device settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Premium Version

The Premium (Pro) version removes all advertisements from the app. Purchasing Premium does not change our data collection practices, except that ad-related tracking will no longer occur.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
